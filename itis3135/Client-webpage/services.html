Our Services

ComfortCare offers a wide range of personalized clinical services delivered with professionalism and compassion.

Skilled Nursing Care

Our licensed nurses provide in-home clinical care including medication administration, wound care, and chronic disease management.

Physical Therapy

Our physical therapists help clients regain strength and mobility after injury, surgery, or hospitalization all from the comfort of home.

Occupational Therapy

We assist with daily living activities through personalized therapy programs aimed at increasing independence.

Home Health Aide Services

Our aides provide personal care, light housekeeping, meal prep, and companionship to support everyday comfort and safety.

Respite and Palliative Care

We offer short term relief for family caregivers and compassionate support for those in end of life care.
