"Your laces are Moose"


Script









Moose are the largest members of the deer family.
Male moose, called bulls, grow antlers each year, shedding them in the winter and regrowing them in the spring.
Moose are excellent swimmers and can dive up to 6 meters (20 feet) underwater to feed on aquatic plants.
Moose have a keen sense of smell, which helps them detect predators and locate food in their forest habitats.
